# Product Overview

TRNAWL is designed for teams that need lightweight project, SOW, and delivery tracking without the overhead of a large project management suite.

## Primary Users

- Delivery managers
- Project coordinators
- Customer success teams
- Operations leads
- Internal service teams

## Jobs To Be Done

- Capture delivery work quickly
- Assign clear owners
- Track due dates and review status
- Identify overdue and at-risk work
- Replace scattered spreadsheet trackers with one shared operating view

## Product Tone

TRNAWL should feel fast, calm, and practical. The experience should be closer to a clean ticket board than a heavy enterprise workflow system.
