# Product Overview

TRNAWL is designed for teams that need lightweight project, SOW, delivery, and customer-action coordination without the overhead of a large project management suite.

The product is about execution visibility. It helps teams coordinate work that currently falls between Jira, ServiceNow, Planner, Excel, Teams, email, and SharePoint.

## Primary Users

- Delivery managers
- Project coordinators
- Customer success teams
- Operations leads
- Internal service teams
- Sales and presales teams
- Solution architects
- Governance reviewers

## Jobs To Be Done

- Capture delivery work quickly
- Assign clear owners
- Track due dates and review status
- Identify overdue and at-risk work
- Replace scattered spreadsheet trackers with one shared operating view
- Turn repeatable SOW and delivery-review processes into lightweight workflows
- Make blockers, missing approvals, and customer actions visible
- Use AI to improve clarity before work enters the system

## Product Tone

TRNAWL should feel fast, calm, and practical. The experience should be closer to a clean ticket board than a heavy enterprise workflow system.

Updating work should feel faster than sending another Teams message. The interface should avoid unnecessary fields, dense administration, and process language that slows adoption.
